Project Site Plan

Site Name

Solo Travel for Women

This website provides resources and guidance for women who travel solo, ensuring they have safe, enjoyable, and empowering experiences.

Optional domain availability: solotravelwomen.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to serve as a guide for women who want to travel alone. It will feature travel tips, safety advice, destination guides, and a community forum for sharing experiences.

Scenarios

  • What are the safest destinations for solo female travelers?
  • How can I meet other solo travelers while staying safe?

Color Schema

  • #2C3E50 (Dark Blue) - Used for headers and navigation.
  • #F39C12 (Golden Yellow) - Used for accents and buttons.

Typography

  • Montserrat - Used for headings.
  • Open Sans - Used for body text.
